Privacy Policy for Melbourne Land Valuator

Last updated: July 2, 2025

Overview

Melbourne Land Valuator is a Chrome extension that calculates and displays price per square meter data on realestate.com.au property listings, with Melbourne suburb median comparisons.

Data Collection

We do not collect any user data. The Melbourne Land Valuator extension:

  • Does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • Does not track browsing history or user activity
  • Does not use cookies or any other tracking mechanisms
  • Does not share any data with third parties

How the Extension Works

The extension functions entirely within your browser and only on realestate.com.au property listing pages. It:

  • Reads property information directly from the webpage you're viewing
  • Performs calculations locally in your browser
  • Uses a static dataset of Melbourne suburb median prices that is included within the extension package
  • Does not send any data to external servers

Permissions

The extension requires access to realestate.com.au property listing pages only to read property data from the page content. This permission is necessary for the extension to function but does not involve collecting or storing any user data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website at https://melb-land-values.netlify.app/.
